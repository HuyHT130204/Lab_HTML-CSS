About Us

Wild Rescues is a nonprofit organization dedicated to helping injured wildlife. Our goal is to help bring these animals back to health and release them back into their natural habitat.

We serve many species of wildlife:

  • Raccoons
  • Squirrels
  • Fox
  • Birds
  • Horses
  • Deer
  • Pigs
  • Reptiles
