About Us

Wild Rescues is a registered nonprofit wildlife rescue and rehabilitation facility, located in Ocala, Florida. We help injured, sick, or orphaned wildlife. Our goal is to rescue, rehabilitate, and release these animals back into their natural environment.

We are a facility licensed by the Florida Fish and Wildlife Conservation Commission, and we provide care in compliance with all federal and state regulations.

Our organization is funded through donations, sponsorship, and grants. We provide guided tours and educational programs for schools, scouts, and other groups by appointment.
