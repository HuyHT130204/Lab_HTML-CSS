Partner with Us

There are many ways that you can partner with Wild Rescues. We need caring volunteers to help care for our animals. You can also donate, become a member, or become a sponsor.

Volunteers

We need volunteers to help care for our animals. Daily tasks include feeding, cleaning enclosures, and providing enrichment activities. We also need volunteers to assist with office work, community outreach, and special events.

Donate

Your donations help us provide food, medical care, and shelter for our animals. We accept monetary donations, as well as supplies from our wishlist.

Membership

Become a member and receive updates about our animals, invitations to special events, and discounts on merchandise.

Sponsorship

Sponsor an animal and help provide for its care. You'll receive updates about your sponsored animal and can schedule visits to see them.
