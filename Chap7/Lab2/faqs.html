Frequently Asked Questions

What should I do if I find injured wildlife?

If you find injured wildlife, please contact us immediately. Do not attempt to handle the animal yourself as this could be dangerous for both you and the animal.

Do you accept all types of wildlife?

We accept most types of wildlife native to Florida. However, we are not equipped to handle venomous snakes or alligators.

How can I volunteer?

Please visit our Partnership page to learn about volunteer opportunities. All volunteers must complete an orientation and training program.

Are you open to the public?

We are not open for drop-in visits, but we do offer guided tours by appointment. Please contact us to schedule a tour.
