About Us

Wild Rescues is a registered nonprofit wildlife rescue and rehabilitation facility, located in Ocala, Florida. We help injured, sick, or orphaned wildlife. Our goal is to rehabilitate and release these animals back into their natural environment.

We are a member of the National Wildlife Rehabilitators Association.

Our Mission

Our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate, and release wildlife back into their natural environment. We provide around-the-clock care for:

  • Injured Animals
  • Sick Animals
  • Orphaned Animals

How You Can Help

You can help us achieve our mission in several ways:

  • Volunteer at our facility
  • Donate Supplies
  • Become a member
  • Become a sponsor
